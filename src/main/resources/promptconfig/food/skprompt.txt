Vegetarian Dish: Potato and Black Bean Patties

Ingredients:
- 4 medium potatoes (boiled and mashed)
- 1 cup black beans (cooked and mashed)
- 1-2 jalapeños (finely chopped, optional)
- 2 cloves garlic (minced)
- 2 tbsp cilantro (finely chopped)
- 2 tbsp cornmeal
- 1/2 tsp chili powder
- 1/2 tsp cumin powder
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- Salt to taste
- Oil for frying

Instructions:
Mix potatoes, beans, jalapeños, garlic, cilantro, cornmeal, and spices. Shape into patties. Fry until golden. Serve with salsa or guacamole.
